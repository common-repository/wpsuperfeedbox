Adds a fancy hideable Feed Box to your Post Page and boosts your subscriber count.

== Description ==

Just place <code>&lt;?php addWPSuperFeedBox(); ?&gt;</code> anywhere in your <code>single.php</code> to start boosting. That's it!

== Screenshots ==

1. View of the Feed Box

== Installation ==

1. Copy folder `wpsuperfeedbox` to your `/wp-content/plugins/` directory
1. Activate the plugin in your admin-panel ('Plugins')
1. Place `<?php addWPSuperFeedBox(); ?>` anywhere in your `single.php`
